Hey!

I’m Dionis, everyone calls me Denis, except for Border Force officers when they see my ID.

I’m a self-taught UI Designer and Front-End Developer.

I first got in contact with coding, when I tried to customize my Blogspot XML template. It was 2007, I was 15. Instead of chasing girls I was turned on day and night by knowing that I do something different, I was making my own pocket money with Adsense. I simply can’t describe the feeling when my mom had to cash out my checks, I wasn’t old enough to do it.

Soon after, I discovered Blogger’s limitations. I abandoned ship and began searching for a better solution. That’s when I found out the great, mighty unlimited possibilities, Wordpress.

Learning Wordpress opened new doors for me. For a period of time, I sold websites on Flippa when Flippa was in her heyday. That provided me with enough money to live independently while I was in high-school. This time I had enough pocket money to go and buy furniture for my rented apartment. I guess the saying “money is power” is true after all.

Besides selling websites, some of the buyers asked me to help them with different web projects.

Flippa’s glory came to an end and that sent me on a new adventure. To provide web development services through Elance (now Upwork). I had the experience, knowledge of building numerous websites, from simple HTML & CSS to Magento shops, Automated Wordpress websites, etc. It was time to cash on what I knew to do.

After a few failed attempts to compete with low bids, soon enough, the first job came in, then second, third and so on. Every client I had were happy with the quality of my services. Of course, to make them feel that way, I sometimes went above and beyond of what was expected from me.

Pretty much all my freelance clients wanted to continue working with me after we got one project done. I ended up having several long-term projects. That led me to the idea of having my own design agency and scale what I had going.

Of course, I tried doing it, but I failed miserably. I found that you need to be a strong leader, salesman, marketer, mentor. And I was none of these, In my mind I was thinking, with enough time I can try teach myself those skills with books and courses. But these, unfortunately, are qualities you develop while doing, it takes a lot of practice and time.

I now moved to the UK, and looking for new challenges.
